Introduction

Saylani Welfare trust is one of the largest NGO in Pakistan. Saylani's mission is to support the underprivileged by providing essential servics such as free food,ration distribution,medical vocational tarning programs, and modern IT education.As a affiliate of Saylani Welfare International Trust in Pakistan,which has been dedicated to humanitarian servics since 1999,we contribute significantly to the well-being of society, investing over a billion rupees montly in various philanthropic endeavors.
